KEY APP FEATURES

Instantly Share More Information

When you meet someone, instantly share more social, business & contact information from phone to phone, with just 1 scan by any smartphone camera*! The person scanning doesn't need to have ContSync.

Exchange Info With ContSync Users

If you meet a ContSync user, use the split screen to scan; then tap Exchange to share your selected card. If that user scans you instead and exchanges a card, save it from Received Cards.

Multiple Contact Cards

ContSync intuitively creates different contact cards from your Profile so you can choose what information you’d like to share with different people. Easily edit or create more cards.

Auto-Updated Phonebook

Enjoy an always up-to-date phonebook with information of contacts in blue updated directly by the source; and you don't lose old information.

Easily Send Card to Meeting Attendees

Just select a meeting from your calendar, the people and the card and send.

Invite Your Contacts to Stay Auto-updated

Invite your contacts to simply download ContSync to have each other’s information auto-updated.
