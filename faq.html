FAQ's

FAQ's

1. When I add/edit a contact in my device’s Contacts or from my default account (Google, iCloud, Outlook etc.), will it show in ContSync?

Yes, it will once you restart the app. You can also manually tap on “Sync Now” from the menu on the Contacts page to pull it in ContSync.

2. What does the Sync button do?

When ContSync is restarted, it captures all changes between your device's Contacts and ContSync. To do it manually, tap Sync.

3. When I add a contact in ContSync, through scanning, Received Card or manually, will it save in my device’s Contacts and in my default account (i.e. Google, iCloud, Outlook etc.)?

When you save a Received Card or manually add a contact, it is automatically added to your device’s Contacts. When you scan someone’s QR code, ContSync will add it to your device’s Contacts once it restarts. You can also manually tap on “Sync" from Contacts menu to push the added contacts into your device’s Contacts.

4. If one of my contact makes a change to their ContSync profile, will it show in my device’s Contacts and will I continue to have the old information in my device’s Contacts?

Yes, the change will reflect in ContSync in short while. Also, you won’t lose the old information. ContSync will simply add to the label of the changed field to state “(Old)”.

5. Why is a field showing in grey/black colour?

It means that this field is not auto-updating from the source contact because ContSync did not find a match or where it was earlier in blue, then that contact has deleted that field or you have manually edited that field.

6. Why is my number showing as already registered?

This means that the number was in use by someone else who had registered with it on ContSync. If this number is now yours (networks circulate unused numbers after a gap), you can “Claim Number” and verify it with the OTP that we send you.

7. Why are some contacts not matching even though I know they are using ContSync?

Once you have some information about a contact which is verified by that contact on ContSync, then ContSync will find a match with other information and keep it auto-updated.

8. Why are some fields not matching/syncing?

That ContSync user may not have that information in their profile.

9. Why is some information from my card not being shared with the person whom I’ve shared a card with?

If the person you have shared it with is not on ContSync or that person has not received the information through ContSync, certain special fields exclusively on ContSync will not be shared.

10. Why are some of my contacts not showing in ContSync?

This could happen with an Android device. Contacts that are stored on your device’s SIM and not on your device’s memory or in a default account (i.e. Google, iCloud, Outlook etc.), are not pulled by ContSync. You can export them into your default account and ContSync will then pull this information.

11. Why can’t I add, modify and delete information in my profile at one go?

Since edits to your Profile are synced with others and to protect your privacy and theirs, edit has been broken up into Add, Modify and Delete.

  • ADD - To protect your privacy, any information that you Add is not shared with anyone, until you choose to do it (by sharing or including it as part of a Card). During Edit -> Add, you cannot Modify or Delete any information.
  • MODIFY - If you modify any information in your Profile, anyone who had that information will be auto-updated with the new. During Edit -> Modify, you cannot Add or Delete any information.
  • DELETE - If you delete information from your Profile, this is not deleted from others who had it, to protect their right to continue to have it. For them it simply turns grey/black and stops auto-updating. During Edit -> Delete, you cannot Add or Modify any information.

12. What cards can I see in the landscape mode?

Cards that you have received during the past 36 hours (under “Cards”) or of participants of a selected meeting (under “Attendees”).

13. Why can’t I change my login number and primary email id through Edit of my profile?

Since this information is linked to your account and needs to be verified, you can do this from Settings -> Account Settings.

14. What permissions are you seeking from me for my device?

Contacts, Camera, Calendar, Background App Refresh, Internet Connectivity (Cellular or Wi-Fi).

15. Why am I not getting the verification email?

Please check your Spam box or add information@contsyncapp.com to your safe account.

16. If I manually merge duplicates, will it also merge on my device’s Contacts?

Yes, it will merge in your device’s Contacts too.

17. Why is a duplicate contact being created?

Duplicates may be created by certain devices or default account or certain apps or where you are using multiple Devices. Sometimes, these are resolved (i.e. duplicates disappear) when the Device syncs with the app or default account. You can use the “Merge Contacts” from the Settings menu to merge duplicates.

18. Why are certain numbers not being pulled by ContSync?

Phone numbers that have special characters in them and contacts that are stored in an account not associated with an email, are not imported by ContSync and will not show in ContSync.
